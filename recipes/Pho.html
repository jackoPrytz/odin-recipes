Vietnamese Pho

Grandma's homemade Pho

Pho is a popular food in Vietnam[6] where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Pho is considered Vietnam's national dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Handful Bean sprouts
  • 1 bunch Fresh coriander
  • 5 fresh chillies
  • 1 lime
  • 500g beef fillet
  • 750ml beef broth
  • 200g rice noodles

Steps

  1. In a medium size saucepan bring you beef broth to the boil this can take 10-15 minutes.
  2. While your broth comes to the boil roughly chop your coriander and chilles, thinly slice your beef and season with salt.
  3. Once you're broth has come to the boil, remove from heat and add noodles and beef and leave for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve into a bowl, garnish with chillies, coriander bean sprouts and a slice of lime.
